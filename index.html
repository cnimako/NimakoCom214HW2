Welcome to Carmen's Beauty Room

Glowing Transformations Await: Step into Our Serene Beauty Haven

The BeautyRoom was created to cater to the POC community in my college. Many PWIs lack diverse hair care options on or near campus. Black students at these institutions struggle to find products and services catering to their hair needs. I noticed this problem and knew I could do something about it so I started a business that fills this gap. POCs attending PWIs often feel underserved or overlooked by mainstream beauty standards prevalent in predominantly white environments. A hair business specifically tailored to their needs can provide a sense of representation and inclusion, fostering a supportive community and enhancing their overall experience at the institution.

About Me
